KKTC Fenerbahçeliler Derneği

  • Foundation Year:

    2006

  • Location:

    KKTC - LEFKOŞA

KKTC Fenerbahçeliler Derneği, TRNC Fenerbahce Association, founded on March 20, 2006 by 100 founding members, the purpose of the association is to gather the fans and sports fans who love Fenerbahçe Sports Club in the TRNC under one roof, to meet the social and cultural needs of the members, to ensure that they become members of the Club, to provide all kinds of material and moral assistance and to work in accordance with these purposes. Ercan İbrahimoğlu is the founding president of the association and İbrahimoğlu served between 2006-2012. Erçin Çoban was the second president of the association between 2012-2018 and served as the chairman for 3 terms. Gökhan Necipoğlu, who took over the duty from Çoban, became the third president of the association by serving as the chairman for a term between 2018-2020. The current president of the TRNC Fenerbahce Association is Toykan Özdamar. The Association Tavern is located on the Küçük Kaymaklı Post Office on Kızılay Street in the capital, Nicosia.
